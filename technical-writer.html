Background

About Me

I specialize in producing mission-critical documentation for systems that demand absolute precision — avionics platforms, unmanned aircraft systems, defense electronics, enterprise software, and big data architectures. My work bridges engineering teams and end users, turning complex system behavior into clear, auditable, and standards-conformant deliverables.

With a Doctor of Computer Science in Big Data Analytics and graduate degrees in engineering and technology management, I bring deep technical fluency to every project. I don't just document systems — I understand them at the architecture level, which means fewer review cycles, fewer errors, and documentation that engineers actually trust.

I've authored and managed documentation programs at Northrop Grumman, Collins Aerospace, Thermo Fisher Scientific, Verimatrix, Engility, and other organizations across the aerospace, defense, IT, and life sciences sectors.
